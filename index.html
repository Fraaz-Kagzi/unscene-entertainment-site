Our flagship production, Echoes of the Past: The Veil, is a haunting historical drama that blends mystery, romance, and atmosphere into a single immersive world. It forms the foundation of our wider creative ecosystem, expanding into music, publishing, and digital storytelling.

Behind the Door

UNSCENE Entertainment is a creative production company based in Blackburn, Lancashire.

Founded in 2025 by Furqan Kagzi and Douy Singsamran, we create original stories rooted in atmosphere, emotion, and the unseen.

Our work is designed to linger long after the lights fade.

Drawn to horror, folklore, and human fragility, we explore the space between history and imagination, memory and myth.

Lancashire gives us more than location. It gives us conflict, folklore, architecture, belief systems, and stories that refuse to disappear.

We build from what already exists here and shape it into work that travels far beyond it.

Ambitious work does not need to come from traditional creative centres to matter.

From stage to page to screen, we transform local heritage into immersive experiences that resonate far beyond where they began.
