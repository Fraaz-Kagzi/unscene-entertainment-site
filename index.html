Behind the Door

UNSCENE Entertainment is a creative production company based in Blackburn, Lancashire, founded in 2025 by Furqan Kagzi and Douy Singsamran.

We create original stories rooted in atmosphere, emotion, and the unseen - work that lingers long after the lights fade. Drawn to horror, folklore, and human fragility, UNSCENE exists to explore the spaces between history and imagination, memory and myth.

Our projects are inspired by Lancashire’s culture, landscapes, and hidden histories. We believe regional stories carry universal weight, and that ambitious, professional work can be created beyond traditional creative centres.

From stage to page to screen, we transform local heritage into immersive experiences that speak to audiences everywhere.

Current focus

Over the coming years, UNSCENE will focus on developing and producing Echoes of the Past: The Veil, while building a sustainable creative pipeline that supports future projects, collaborations, and opportunities for regional artists.

Long-term vision

Our long-term aim is to create work that lives beyond a single production, expanding across stage, page, and digital platforms, and contributing to a lasting creative legacy in the North of England.
