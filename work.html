The Work We Bring to Light

UNSCENE develops and produces original theatrical, literary, and visual work shaped by strong storytelling, distinctive visual identity, and emotional resonance

What we explore

Our projects often explore themes of love, loss, belief, identity, and legacy, using genre, particularly horror and gothic drama, as a gateway to human connection.

Flagship Production

Echoes of the Past: The Veil

Our flagship production, Echoes of the Past: The Veil, is a haunting historical drama blending mystery, romance, and atmosphere into a single immersive world.

The project forms the foundation of UNSCENE’s wider creative ecosystem, expanding into music, publishing, and digital storytelling, and supporting our long-term sustainability as a creative company.

Work in progress
