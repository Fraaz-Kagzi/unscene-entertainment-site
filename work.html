The Work We Bring to Light

UNSCENE develops and produces original theatrical, literary, and visual work built on strong storytelling and distinctive visual identity.

We return again and again to themes of love, loss, belief, identity, and legacy. Genre, particularly horror and gothic drama, becomes a doorway into human connection.

We create for audiences who are drawn to atmosphere and emotion. For those who value stories that linger, question, and stay with them long after the experience ends.

Flagship Production

Echoes of the Past: The Veil

Our flagship production, Echoes of the Past: The Veil, is a haunting historical drama that blends mystery, romance, and atmosphere into a single immersive world. It forms the foundation of our wider creative ecosystem, expanding into music, publishing, and digital storytelling.
